Turmeric and cardamom granola with mango lassi yogurt

We often repeat the same breakfast each morning, body and brain on autopilot. Sometimes it’s good to mix it up. This granola uses just seeds and nuts - no oats or grains - and is held together with coconut oil and maple syrup. It’s a great base recipe - add whatever needs using up from the store cupboard; pecans, chia or hemp can go into the mix before roasting; dates, raisins, apricots and prunes can be chopped and mixed through after the granola is out of the oven. The lassi yogurt recipe makes enough for two people, as it is best made fresh. Add more lime if you like it tart or honey to sweeten it.

Prep time

10 minutes

Cook time

1 hr

Serves

Lassi serves 2
